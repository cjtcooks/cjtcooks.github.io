Cha is love. Cha is life. This is a classic drink we have in Bangladesh during breakfast and during whenever. Here, I am writing two variants of it: a classic plain cha and a masala cha recipe. You can drink cha with biscuits or bread or buns! Just dip them in! :3

Directions

    To make the plain cha:
  • restaurant Add the milk and water to a pot.
  • restaurant Keep the pot in medium heat and bring the milk to boil.
  • restaurant Add the black tea and take the pot off of the burner.
  • restaurant Gently stir the tea until the classic tea color is achieved. Serve it with a biscuit, bread, or bun!
    To make the masala cha:
  • restaurant When adding the milk and water in the plain cha recipe, just add the masalas (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, saffron (optional)) from the ingredients list and continue with the rest of the steps.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Milk 4 cups
Water 1 cup
Cardamom 6
Cinnamon Stick 2
Cloves 4
Fresh Ginger 1 small cube
Black Tea 4-6 tbsp
Sugar By taste
Servings 2 people
